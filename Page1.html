About Me

My name is Julien Dupas (located on the right) and I have had a passion for filmmaking for several years now alongside my twin brother Liam (located on the left)
Since highschool began, I took my first Audio Video Film and Technology class.
The spark for creation caught my attention.
I filmed my first projects in class with studio
cameras with the help of some peers. I was really interested in the process of filming and
editing. After my first project, I downloaded Premiere Pro which is an industry standard software.
At my house and began to dive deeper into editing in my free time.
I taught myself Premiere by reading, watching tutorials, and most importantly trial and error.
I have since created numerous passion projects ranging from short films, documentaries and music videos.
